In-Game Leader Eligibility:

Fulfill one criteria from the following section.

  • actively enrolled or successfully completed one full ESEA-MDL season as the In-Game Leader;
  • actively enrolled or successfully completed one full ESEA-Advanced season as the In-Game Leader;
  • actively enrolled or successfully completed one full ESEA-Main season as the In-Game Leader; or
  • actively enrolled as an in-game leader on a top 15 accredited collegiate team according to NACCS League Rankings.
